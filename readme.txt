It allows you to track what users are searching in your woocommerce store, wordpress blog or website.

== Description ==
This plugin allows you to **track** what customers / clients are searching when they visit your **Woocommerce Store** or **Wordpress Blog** or **Wordpress Site**.
It will tell you exactly what any individual searched on your site and how many times anything got searched, It will give you the complete records.

= Why to try =
* Know what people are searching in you woocommerce store
* Know what people are searching in your blog
* Know how many times anything got searched in your store / site / blog
* Increase sells
* Increase followers
* Increase readers
* Increase visits of people by providing what they looking for

= Benefits =
* You will be able to increase the sells, since you know what people are exactly looking for.
* you can increase the visits of the readers / followers if you are running a blog.
